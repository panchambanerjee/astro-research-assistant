# Hypothesis Generation

Generate concrete, testable astrophysics research hypotheses.

Each hypothesis must include:
- hypothesis claim
- motivation from literature
- supporting papers
- proposed test
- required data
- required method
- falsification criteria
- novelty score 1-5
- testability score 1-5
- data availability score 1-5
- impact score 1-5
- difficulty score 1-5
- already-done risk 1-5

Avoid vague hypotheses.
